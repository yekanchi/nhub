You can help us to translate BitComet into your own language.

1. Please visit http://www.bbcomet.com/projects/bitcomet/ and try to translate on the web.

2. After finish translation, you can download .mo file from the website, and save it to this folder
   to have a try. BitComet will load it after restart.

3. When the next release of BitComet, we will merge your translation into the install package.

Thank you for your support.

.mo file from above translate website, and save it to this folder. BitComet will load it
after restart.
